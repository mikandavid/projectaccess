School partnerships

Free programmes for schools.

Many students learn too late what is possible. Project Access Austria brings students and alumni of international universities directly to Austrian schools — free of charge and tailored to each school.

Enquire about a school partnership

What we offer

What schools receive.

  • School visits by students and alumni of international universities.
  • Webinars for final-year classes or interested year groups.
  • Materials on studying abroad, applications and funding.
  • A personal contact in the PA Austria team.
  • Session planning by audience, format and focus.

Possible topics

Topics for school sessions.

  • Applications in the UK, the US and Europe
  • Choosing courses and universities that fit
  • Personal statement, essay and interviews
  • Scholarships, funding and costs abroad
  • Studying in Austria vs. abroad

Contact

Enquiries and contact.

Write to us with the relevant subject line — we reply within a few days.
