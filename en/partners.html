Partners

Partners help keep our work free.

With schools, companies, foundations and private supporters, we reach young people who often hear about international study options too late. Partners can contribute space, funding, expertise or contacts.

Forms of support

How partners can help.

Funding

Fund the bootcamp and mentoring.

Sponsorship helps cover accommodation, meals, materials and travel costs. That means applicants can take part even if these costs would otherwise be too high.

Spaces

Spaces for workshops and information evenings.

Schools, universities and companies help us run free sessions where they are needed.

Expertise

Know-how for workshops and interviews.

Partners contribute practical expertise, for example in workshops with McKinsey or Schwarzman Scholars.

Network

Connections to schools, alumni and funders.

A stronger network helps our free support reach relevant applicants faster.

School partnerships

Free sessions for schools.

Many students only hear late about study options abroad and how the applications work. Project Access Austria brings students and alumni of international universities directly to Austrian schools, free of charge and tailored to what each school needs.

Enquire about a school partnership

What we offer

What schools get.

  • School visits with students and alumni of international universities.
  • Webinars for final-year classes or interested year groups.
  • Materials on studying abroad, applications and funding.
  • A personal contact from our PA Austria team.
  • Session planning adapted to the audience, format and focus.

Possible topics

Possible topics for school sessions.

  • Applications in the UK, the US and Europe
  • Choosing courses and universities that fit
  • Personal statement, essay and interviews
  • Scholarships, funding and costs abroad
  • Studying in Austria vs. abroad

Contact

Enquiries and contact.

Write to us with the relevant subject line. We usually reply within a few days.
