Project Access Austria

International universities — regardless of background.

Project Access Austria offers students from Austria and South Tyrol free one-to-one mentoring and an annual application bootcamp. Mentors are students and alumni of international universities.

About Project Access

An international mentoring network.

Project Access is an international non-profit with more than 5,000 mentors worldwide. Project Access Austria connects motivated young people from Austria and South Tyrol with students and alumni at Oxford, Cambridge, Harvard, ETH Zürich, Sciences Po, Bocconi and other universities.

Programme

Programme overview.

  1. One-to-one mentoring

    A personal mentor for feedback, shared experience and concrete next steps.

  2. Application bootcamp

    Four days of admissions knowledge, essay coaching, interview training and exchange.

  3. Courses & resources

    Course choice, funding, essays, interviews — structured preparation and follow-up.

  4. Community & network

    Other motivated applicants from Austria and South Tyrol with similar goals.
