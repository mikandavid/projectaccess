What we run

Four formats throughout the year.

From a one-hour talk to a multi-day bootcamp – formats are adapted to the audience and time frame.

School visits

Talks at schools across Austria.

Students and alumni of international universities visit classes, answer questions first-hand and give a realistic picture of study paths abroad.

Webinars

Online sessions on applications and funding.

Topics like course choice, personal statements, scholarships and selection processes in 60–90 minutes, with live questions.

Bootcamp

Four days of intensive application training.

Once a year in Lower Austria. Small groups, mock interviews and concrete feedback – including accommodation and meals.

Education fairs

Presence at fairs such as BeSt in Vienna.

Students, parents and teachers can speak with us directly at the stand about studying abroad and applications.

Upcoming dates

Where new dates appear first.

Concrete dates for webinars, school visits and fairs are announced on Instagram and in our newsletter. Schools can request a dedicated session at any time.
