Becoming a mentor

Mentoring in the international Project Access programme.

Mentors support students from Austria and South Tyrol on essays, interviews and selection. You don't need perfect answers — experience, honest feedback and time are enough.

Requirements

Mentoring fits if you

  • are from Austria or South Tyrol, or are familiar with the educational context
  • are studying or have studied at an international university
  • can give constructive feedback and want to invest time
  • consider fair university access important
